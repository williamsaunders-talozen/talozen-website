# Features

### Features (ignore for now)

Based on the provided sources, here is a comprehensive feature list for the Talozen rental management platform, categorized by functionality:

**1\. Automated Workflow & Process Management**

* **End-to-End Automation:** The platform provides fully automated flows for three core real estate processes: Mandates, New Leases, and Renewals.  
* **Mandate Flow:** Automates the process from sending a link to the owner, receiving the application and approval, digitally signing the mandate, and listing the property.  
* **New Lease Flow:** manages the tenant journey from application link, credit check fee payment, approval, lease fee payment, to final digital signature.  
* **Renewal Flow:** Streamlines renewals through approval, digital links, renewal fee payments, and signing.  
* **Task Triggers:** Steps are automated so that actions trigger the next phase; for example, once a payment is made, the lease is automatically sent for signature.

**2\. Digital Contracting & Documentation**

* **Zero Paperwork:** Eliminates physical forms and filing by digitizing the administrative process,.  
* **One-Click Contract Generation:** Draft contracts are generated instantly using accurate property and tenant information stored in the system.  
* **Digital Signatures:** Mandates and lease agreements can be sent to one or multiple parties for digital signature via email or WhatsApp.  
* **Secure Storage:** All documents are stored digitally in secure cloud storage.

**3\. Tenant & Lead Management**

* **Unlimited Applications:** The system accepts unlimited tenant applications automatically, operating 24/7,.  
* **Lead Tracking:** Consolidates leads from multiple sources into one place with tools to track lead sources and effectiveness.  
* **Application Progress:** Agents can view the granular status of a lead, such as "Link sent," "Application details," "Document upload," "Banking details," or "Fee payment".  
* **Tenant Grading:** Administrators can click a button to retrieve credit check reports and grade tenants to prepare them for landlord review.

**4\. Inventory & Data Management (CMS)**

* **Centralized Database:** Acts as a Content Management System (CMS) where property, owner, and tenant records are linked, allowing users to view all related agreements in one place,.  
* **Inventory History:** Users can track empty units, view inventory history, and read comments associated with specific properties.  
* **Live Updates:** Property, rental amounts, and owner information can be updated instantly and synchronized across documents,.

**5\. Compliance & Security**

* **FICA Readiness:** Tenant applications are processed to be "FICA ready" and fully FICAed for landlords at scale.  
* **Company information gathering:** Gather details for complex  Juristic entities, such as nested ownership structures, from the owner. Easily gather all owner FICA documentation.  
* **POPIA Compliance:** The document management system is built to remain compliant with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).  
* **Property Condition Reports:** The system can automatically generate property condition reports.

**6\. Dashboards & Oversight**

* **Real-Time Analytics:** Customizable dashboards track applications, occupancy rates, and financials using live data.  
* **Agent Performance Tracking:** Management can track agent revenue and performance to ensure oversight of the "book",.  
* **Selfie Lookup:** The interface includes a visual "Selfie lookup" feature to easily identify tenants and agents within the dashboard,.  
* **Guard Rails:** The system includes built-in guard rails to reduce data entry errors and ensure process consistency.
